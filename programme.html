Programme

The EMSAC will take place from the 5th to the 9th of June. Wednesday the 5th of June is the day of arrival and will start with a “Welcome aboard”-dinner.

Thursday morning the students will follow lectures, and receive a tour through the university. They will see the 3mE faculty, including the towing tank.

Friday is excursion day. During this day the students will be introduced to all of the facets that the Dutch Maritime Industry has to offer. This will be done by going on excursions to important Dutch maritime companies This day will have a spectacular end: after a dinner in the student houses where the international students sleep, they will dance the night away at the biggest Froude party of the year: Froudelicious!

Saturday, the students will travel to Amsterdam for a day of culture. They will attend a boat ride through the canals of Amsterdam, visit the Maritime Museum and have free time to explore the city. This day will end with a closing dinner.

Sunday is the end of the conference. The international students will travel back to their respective home countries on this day.
