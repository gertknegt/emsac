Organization

In 1992 two members of the board of William Froude invited shipbuilding students for the first EMSAC (The FEMSAC). This year William Froude is organizing it again. Instead of two people in 1992, six people (including one board member) from S.G. “William Froude” are organizing the EMSAC in 2013. They started in November 2012 with gathering ideas and organizing the EMSAC week. The committee members are all very enthusiast to make this EMSAC a big success!
