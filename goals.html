Goals

The committee has several goals we want to achieve by organizing the EMSAC. Firstly, we would like to stimulate international contact between Maritime students. Other goals are:

  • Give students the opportunity to get to know fellow students from other countries and cultures,
  • Stimulate a better understanding of the differences and similarities between universities in Europe,
  • Allow students to get acquainted with a few Dutch companies which play a crucial role in the Dutch and international maritime market, thus increasing the students’ international network Lower the threshold for students to study abroad.

We incorporate these goals in making a varied programme that is both stimulating and entertaining. More about this can be read under ‘programme’.
