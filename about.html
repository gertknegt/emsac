S.G."William Froude"

William Froude is the study association of Naval Architecture and Maritime Technology at the Delft University of Technology. The association was founded on the 17th of November 1903 and has been involved in monitoring and improving the quality of the education. Next to this, organizing excursions, guest lectures, social activities and other means to support its members in their academic career have been an important part of its existence.

S.G. ‘William Froude’ offers its member the opportunity to discover many aspects, possibilities and developments of the maritime industry in the Netherlands and abroad.

Every Academic year, the association is led by a board of five members: President, Secretary, Treasurer, Commissioner of Education and Commissioner of Excursions. The board finds its support from the members in the form of committees organizing various events.

More information about the association can be found on the website: www.froude.nl

Organization

In 1992 two members of the board of William Froude invited shipbuilding students for the first EMSAC (The FEMSAC). This year William Froude is organizing it again. Instead of two people in 1992, six people (including one board member) from S.G. “William Froude” are organizing the EMSAC in 2013. They started in November 2012 with gathering ideas and organizing the EMSAC week. The committee members are all very enthusiast to make this EMSAC a big success!
